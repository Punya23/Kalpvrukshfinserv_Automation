If customer says "Haan planning karta hoon" OR "Mere investments hain":

"Bahut log paise toh bachate hain, lekin unhe yeh clear nahi hota ki aage aane wale bade kharchon ke liye wo sufficient hai ya nahi."
OR
"Kabhi kabhi sirf ek expert ki choti si salah se kaafi clarity mil jaati hai ki sab kuch sahi direction mein chal raha hai ya nahi."

## STAGE 3.5 — RECOVERY SYSTEM (LOW AWARENESS)
